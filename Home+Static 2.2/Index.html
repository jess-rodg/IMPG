The Tayal are found on both sides of the Central Mountain Range in northern Taiwan, as well as in the mountainous areas of Hualien and Yilan. They are divided into the Tayal and the Sedek. The Tayal are divided into the Sekoleq and Tseole groups. The Sedek subgroup is divided into the Eastern Sedek and Western Sedek groups.
